Mol* (/'molstar/) is an open collaboration started by PDBe and RCSB PDB to provide a technology stack for data delivery and analysis tools of macromolecules.

Join Development on GitHub


Mol* Viewer
Macromolecular online viewer capable of coordinate and experimental data streaming and their visualisation, along with the ability to display quality, functional or biological context annotations. Most notable features include the ability to simultaneously and interactively visualise up to hundreds of (superimposed) protein structures; play molecular dynamics trajectories, or render cell-level models at atomic detail with tens of millions of atoms.
